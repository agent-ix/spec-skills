# CC1 - Control Environment

**Category**: Common Criteria  
**Focus**: Governance, integrity, and organizational structure

## When to Apply

CC1 controls are organizational-level and typically addressed by:
- Company policies
- Management practices
- HR and organizational structure

**For software components**: CC1 is usually inherited from organizational policies rather than implemented in code. Document that organizational CC1 controls apply.

## Controls

| Control | Name | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| CC1.1 | Integrity and Ethics | Organization demonstrates commitment to integrity and ethical values |
| CC1.2 | Board Oversight | Board of directors demonstrates independence and exercises oversight |
| CC1.3 | Management Structure | Management establishes structures, reporting lines, authorities |
| CC1.4 | Competence Commitment | Organization demonstrates commitment to attract, develop, retain competent individuals |
| CC1.5 | Accountability | Organization holds individuals accountable for their internal control responsibilities |

## Mapping Guidance

For most software components, CC1 maps to:
- **StR-XXX**: Stakeholder requirements about governance
- **NFR-XXX**: Non-functional requirements about compliance

If no specific requirements exist, document: "CC1 controls addressed at organizational level."
